Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply for both positions?

Yes, you can apply for both positions if you meet the eligibility criteria for each role. However, we recommend focusing on the position that best matches your skills and interests.

Is there any application fee?

No, there is no application fee for any position at Zensora Tech. All our recruitment processes are completely free for applicants.

What is the selection process timeline?

The complete selection process typically takes 1-2 weeks from the application deadline. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted via email or phone.

Are these remote positions?

Campus Ambassador positions are primarily campus-based with remote coordination. Promotion Executive positions offer flexible options - both remote and on-site opportunities are available.

Will I receive training?

Yes, all selected candidates will receive comprehensive training about our services, processes, and their specific role responsibilities before they begin.
