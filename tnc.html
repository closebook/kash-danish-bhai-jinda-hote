1. Operational Distinction and Service Classification

It is of paramount importance to explicitly define the nature of the engagement between the applicant and Zensora Tech. The internship program is open-access and participation-based. Certification and formal recognition are merit-based and issued only upon successful completion and evaluation of assigned tasks.

However, to facilitate the delivery of this program, we must distinguish between the professional opportunity and the administrative processing required to support it. The internship role is not for sale; rather, all fees charged are strictly for certification, administrative processing, evaluation, record verification, and certificate issuance, not for the internship offer itself.

This clear separation ensures that the candidate is evaluated on skill, while the logistical costs are handled through a separate, tangible processing fee.

2. Administrative Verification and Code Review Costs

Upon the participant's request for certification after successful completion of the internship, Zensora Tech incurs immediate operational costs related to the management of the assessment workflow. We are explicitly charging for the administrative cost of verifying the user's code and reviewing task submissions.

This process requires significant resources to ensure that every submission is logged, checked for authenticity, and processed within our systems. The user acknowledges that this fee compensates the platform for the specific administrative overhead involved in tracking their progress and validating the technical integrity of their work throughout the program duration.

3. Allocation for Certification and ID Generation

A primary component of this fee is allocated to the formal credentialing process. Specifically, we are charging for the administrative expense of generating a unique ID for every participant and processing their final certification. This fee covers the setup required to issue a valid, tracked, and serialized certificate upon completion.

By remitting the nominal amount of Ninety-Nine Indian Rupees (₹99), the user is covering the direct costs associated with establishing their unique identity within our records and the subsequent generation of their professional credentials.

Administrative Fee Details

The ₹99 fee covers the following administrative costs:

  • Establishing unique identity within our records
  • Technical verification of submitted work
  • Generation of professional credentials
  • Certificate serialization and tracking
  • Administrative processing and documentation
