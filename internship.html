Frequently Asked Questions

Is the internship paid or unpaid?

We offer both paid and unpaid internship opportunities. Paid positions are based on performance, skills, and project requirements. All interns receive certification and valuable experience.

Can I apply for multiple domains?

Yes, you can apply for multiple domains, but we recommend focusing on one primary area to demonstrate your commitment and expertise.

What is the duration of the internship?

Internships typically range from 3 to 6 months, depending on the domain and project requirements. Flexible durations can be discussed during the interview.

Will I get a certificate after completion?

Yes, all interns who successfully complete their internship program will receive a certificate of completion along with a letter of recommendation.
